About Me

I do a lot of exploratory programming and take a lot of notes about what I work on. Otherwise I'll forget everything!

In 2022, I started converting some of those notes into blog posts. My goal in doing so is to share information that is extremely useful for people who are interested in very specific topics that are not necessarily widely discussed online.

Posts are written in my free time and never reflect the views of my employer.

Feel free to contact me with questions or comments.
